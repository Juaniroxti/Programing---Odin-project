What is an HTML tag?
It's the building blocks that define the structure of the website, like the basis of the webpage.
What are the three parts of an HTML element?
the opening tag, the content, the closing tag
What are void elements, and how are they different from regular HTML elements?
The  are elements that have nothing in them and they don't have a closing tag which means they can't wrap content like a normal tag would. Examples of them are <br> and <img>.
